Jeff Bogue

Dr. Jeff Bogue is the senior pastor of Grace Church of Greater Akron, where he has served with his wife, Heidi, since 1993.

Jeff has a deep passion for his community and for the local church to be involved in making the hope of God tangible to every person. He travels nationally and abroad speaking and training ministry leaders.

Jeff’s proudest titles are that of husband and father. He claims he is married to the most beautiful woman God has ever graced the earth with. They have been married 25 years and are still best buddies. They have five sons and one daughter. Jeff loves the Ohio State Buckeyes and country music.

Joe Caruso

Joe and his wife began attending Grace in July of 2008 and he is currently Lead Pastor of Give It Away at the Bath Campus.

At Bath, Joe is the volunteer guy. He works to connect and develop the needs and the dreams of the church, the inner-city and the world with the passions, gifts, and skills of the people from Grace.

Joe and Mandy have been married for 13 years. They have one daughter, Ainsley, and two dogs, Rory and Napoleon. They love having any kind of fun together, from bike rides and the random kayak trip, to sitting and watching a good TV series.

Ron Kent

Ron Kent has served in various leadership positions in church and parachurch ministry since 2009.

Ron has been a part of 3 church plant teams and is currently an elder at Citizens Akron. He is also the Ministry Director for a youth mentoring and discipleship non-profit called South Akron Youth Mentorship.

He, his wife Ariel, his son Ronnie III, and daughter Alora Joy, live in Akron, Ohio. As a family they are passionate about seeing God’s church become as healthy and true to form as Jesus intended it to be.

Brooke

Brooke, twenty-five, has lived her twenties out in the beautiful country of Haiti. Over the last year she has managed two businesses in Haiti, Bèljoy and Rosie's Boutique, which create jobs and empower Haitian mamas. Currently, she is co-founding Hesed Women's Ministries, planned to launch later this year. Brooke has a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree from Clark Summit University in Organizational Leadership.

Nathan

Nathan is the founder and the director of SEEDnet. During his years as the youth pastor at Ashland Grace in Ohio, the Lord led Nathan and some teens to a low-income apartment complex in Ashland to do "church" with the many kids wandering around. Through that, Nathan then gathered adults for an evangelistic Bible study, which was the beginning of EastGate Bible Church, Nathan's first church plant in 2006.

Since then, Nathan has felt passionate about planting and multiplying churches in low-income, (S.E.E.D.) oppressed areas. Nathan believes that proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus to unbelievers is the key to starting new churches.

Nathan and his wife, Jena, have written a workbook entitled, Gospeling: A Personal Guide To Sharing The Gospel to empower all Christians to verbalize the Good News. They live with their three crazy boys in rural Ashland with their dogs, sheep, chickens, and horse.
